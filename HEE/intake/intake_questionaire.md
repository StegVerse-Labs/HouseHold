# Household Escalation Intake

## Incident Basics
1. What item failed or underperformed?
2. When did the issue first occur?
3. Where did it occur?

## Counterparty
4. Who is responsible (company, contractor, institution)?
5. What obligation applied (warranty, contract, service)?

## Impact
6. Was the item unusable? For how long?
7. Was any mitigation provided (loaner, substitute)?
8. What risks or harms resulted (safety, financial, access)?

## Documentation
9. Do you have proof of purchase?
10. Do you have warranty/guarantee terms?
11. Do you have photos/videos?
12. Are there third-party records (CARFAX, invoices, statements)?

## Desired Outcome
13. What would a reasonable resolution look like?
   (documentation, repair, compensation, extension, clarification)

> This intake produces a Level-0 evidence packet and determines
> the appropriate escalation profile.
