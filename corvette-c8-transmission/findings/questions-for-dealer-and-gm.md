# Questions for Dealer and GM

1. Was the transmission replaced or repaired?
2. If replaced, provide:
   - part number
   - revision/model identifier
   - serial number (if applicable)
   - whether new or remanufactured
3. Provide the full RO history (open/close dates, notes, sublet notes).
4. Explain any mismatch between repair documentation and installed components.
5. Clarify the CARFAX "normal maintenance" entry:
   - what service
   - who performed it
   - why it appears during custody window
6. Confirm whether any technical service bulletins applied at time of repair.
